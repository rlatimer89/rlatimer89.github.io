My Work

Virtual Pet Game

In this Java-based virtual pet care simulation, players assume the role of responsible caretakers for their digital companions. The game challenges players to diligently tend to their pets' needs, encompassing feeding, hydration, and playtime activities. Neglecting these vital responsibilities may result in the pets running away, fostering a sense of accountability and engagement. The virtual pets' well-being is gauged through metrics like hunger, thirst, and happiness, offering players an immersive experience. If these metrics fall to critical levels due to neglect, the game concludes with a "game over" scenario, reinforcing the significance of active care. You can find the source code for this game on GitHub: GitHub Repository.

Car Garage

In the concept of a Java-based car garage game, players are immersed in a dynamic environment that simulates the operations of a virtual car dealership.

This interactive experience enables users to explore a range of exciting functionalities, including the ability to purchase, sell, and even test drive an array of vehicles.

With a focus on realism and authenticity, the game offers a comprehensive inventory of cars, each with unique attributes and pricing, and a robust financial system that reflects the player's financial management skills.

As players buy, sell, and test drive cars, they accumulate wealth, expand their garage, and work toward becoming a successful automotive entrepreneur.

The game's immersive graphics and user-friendly interface enhance the overall gaming experience, creating an engaging and educational platform for enthusiasts of the automotive world and aspiring entrepreneurs.

You can find the source code for this game on GitHub: GitHub Repository.
